# KPI

## KPI 1 - Default Rate

Formula:

Default Rate = Bad Loans / Total Loans

Result:

30 %

Interpretation:

30% of clients represent an increased credit risk.

## Insight 2 - Age vs Loan Risk

Average age of good credit clients: 36.22 years

Average age of bad credit clients: 33.96 years

Difference: 2.26 years

Conclusion:

Younger clients show slightly higher credit risk.

## Insight 3 - Credit Amount vs Loan Risk

Average credit amount for good credit clients: 2,985

Average credit amount for bad credit clients: 3,938

Difference: 953

Conclusion:

Clients with higher loan amounts show significantly higher credit risk.
